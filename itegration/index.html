MAGENTO development

ITEGRATION’s specialty is delivering full end-to-end solutions for Retailers, eCommerce companies and Non Profit Organizations. Our technical expertise delivering Magento Enterprise sites combined with our strong project management and governance is a powerful blend for retailers and fundraisers looking to leverage current assets to drive revenues.

Magento’s features, customizations, and integration possibilities make it the most dynamic enterprise level eCommerce framework available. Magento is robust, flexible and scalable and one of the most effective eCommerce platforms available due to innovative features like mobile templates, shopper filtering and multi-store/site functionality. ITEGRATION clients prefer Magento’s product display, mobile templates, loyalty programs and user account management. Get in contact with ITEGRATION to take advantage of all the exciting possibilities Magento has to offer your operation.
