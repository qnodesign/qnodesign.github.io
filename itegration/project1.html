Product Description

Good360’s innovative marketplace platform, GivingPlace360, brings nonprofits, companies and individual supporters together in a “Circle of Good”, allowing them to work together and to do good more efficiently and effectively than ever before.

Project Description

GOOD360 chose ITEGRATION to develop GivingPlace360 on the Magento Enterprise Platform. ITEGRATION’s business analysts and UI designers collaborated with Good360 leadership to collaboratively design and architect GivingPlace360. Our talented development and testing teams implemented the full Giving Place Platform as a greenfield project, integrating Magento with NetSuite ERP and launching in 2014.

ITEGRATION has since extended GivingPlace360 to Good360 Australia, and continues to support Good360 on a number of eCommerce initiatives.
